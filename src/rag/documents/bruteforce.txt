Brute Force attacks involve systematically trying every possible combination of passwords, passphrases, or encryption keys until the correct one is found.

Impacts:
- Unauthorized account access
- Data breaches
- Account lockouts and denial of service
- Server performance degradation due to high login traffic

Mitigations:
- Account lockout policies after multiple failed attempts
- Strong multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Implementing CAPTCHAs on login interfaces
- Rate limiting login requests by IP address
